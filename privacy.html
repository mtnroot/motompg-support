Privacy Policy for MotoMPG

Effective Date: October 2025

Thank you for using MotoMPG. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

  • User account information – when you sign in, we may collect your email address (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Usage data – we store app data you create (such as vehicle logs, fuel entries, trip data) in a secure Firebase Firestore database.
  • Device information – Firebase may automatically collect basic device info for analytics and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve the MotoMPG app
  • To store your log entries securely in the cloud
  • To propagate the global data for comparison with other users
  • To fix bugs and monitor app performance (via Firebase)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your data is shared only with trusted service providers (Google Firebase) to support app functionality.

4. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account remains active. You can request deletion by contacting us.

5. Security

All data is transmitted securely over HTTPS and stored in Firebase’s encrypted databases.

6. Your Rights

You may request a copy or deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@mtnroot.com.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: hello@mtnroot.com

8. Third-Party Services

MotoMPG uses Google Firebase for authentication, cloud storage, and analytics. By using the app, you agree to Firebase’s privacy terms.
